A course different to all others

The objective is that you acquire financial education and that you can create and manage your own investment portfolio in a simple and fun way..
You can invest in real companies, in real markets, with real prices using a simulation account with virtual money. You can practice investments competing against your friends or against the market and learn in a real environment. You will acquire the necessary financial knowledge by practicing in a real investment simulation, with all the support of our Economics and Investments course that helps you understand market movements and the Economy.

Learn with real practice

The best way to learn is by doing. Once you get the basics you can start to carry out operations in a safe environment, but equal to that of the financial markets, using prices and real market information. In this way you will be able to become familiar with the definition of strategies, the load of operations and the analysis of the results and the correction and adjustment of the same in a safe environment, being able to make mistakes and training for the real world.

Build your investments portfolio with real data

You can create an investment portfolio according to your profile as an investor, This will help you train in the real market. Combining investment knowledge with real examples, you will increase your confidence and understand the results, interpret why they occur, and improve your strategies.
